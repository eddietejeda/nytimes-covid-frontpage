Important Dates

  • December 6, 2019 - The first documented COVID-19 hospital admission.1
  • January 9, 2020 - WHO reported that Chinese authorities determined that the outbreak is caused by a novel coronavirus.2
  • January 28, 2020 - The first time COVID-19 gets top billing on front page.
  • February 11, 2020 - WHO announced that the disease caused would be named COVID-19. 2

What is this site?

I want to track how coverage of COVID-19 changes over time and pinpoint precisely when the virus stops being front-page news. More directly, I want to know when we will have three consecutive days where COVID-19-related news is not front-page news. This is a simple implementation that downloads a PDF of the New York Times front page and looks for the following keywords: corona, covid, virus, pandemic, wuhan.
